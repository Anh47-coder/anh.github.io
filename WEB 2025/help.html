Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour by visiting our Reservation page and selecting your preferred tour package.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking by contacting our support team at least 48 hours before your scheduled tour.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 5 or more. Please contact us for more details.
